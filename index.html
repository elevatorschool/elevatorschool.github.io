About EES

Founded by a QEI licensed veteran of the NYC elevator trade who holds inspection licenses in multiple states, EES strives to arm the elevator industry with the knowledge required to enhance the safety of both the riding public, and the elevator tradespeople.

Our examination preparation classes offer industry-leading instruction on the effective use of national and jurisdictional elevator code, and include our own comprehensive practice tests.

Services we offer

Working closely with your team to ensure your needs are met, EES offers on-site educational classes customizable to your company, union, or general membership:

  • QEI Examination Preparation Classes
  • Jusdictional Licensing Examination Preparation Classes
  • Navigating Elevator Code Classes
  • Safety Classes
  • Troubleshooting Classes (NYC Only)
